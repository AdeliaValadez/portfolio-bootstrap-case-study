Visilant

Website Overhaul Shines Light on Affordable Eyecare Mission with AI and Tech

Concept: Redesign Visilant’s current landing page into a comprehensive multi-page design.

Goals:

  • Provide clarity to the company mission
  • Build credibility
prototype.
Background

Revolutionizing Affordable Eyecare with AI and Tech: Visilant stands at the forefront of innovation, dedicated to reshaping the landscape of affordable eyecare through the power of artificial intelligence and cutting-edge technology. With a mission deeply rooted in accessibility and compassion, Visilant leverages its expertise to illuminate pathways to clearer vision for all. Through a comprehensive website overhaul, we shine a spotlight on their commitment to providing clarity not only in vision but also in their company mission. Join us as we embark on a journey to unveil the transformative impact of Visilant's vision, one pixel at a time.

Problem

Visilant's website faces usability challenges, including an unintuitive header, awkward scrolling, and a lack of features, hindering user engagement and mission communication. How can we enhance usability and accessibility to transform our website into an effective tool for fostering empathy, engagement, and support for their mission?

Our Solution

User-Centric Design:User-Centric Design: Craft an intuitive and user-friendly interface that ensures a seamless navigation experience for visitors, encouraging them to explore the organization's mission and impact.

Content Enrichment: Transform the existing landing page into a multi-dimensional website with dedicated sections, partnerships, and the technology-driven solutions that propel the organization's mission forward.

Build Credibility: Establish trust and credibility by showcasing the organization's achievements and partnerships. Showcase the human stories behind the organization’s impactful initiatives.

Responsive Design: Ensure that the website is accessible across various devices, catering to a diverse audience and enabling easy engagement from different regions and demographics

User Interviews

Research Objective: Identify pain points in the website's UI and improve it for the benefit of the nonprofit.

Goal:Conduct 4 User Interviews to target user pain points regarding website navigation and connection to the organization’s mission

>

Our UX design team transcribed qualitative data from user interviews onto notes, we created a dynamic representation of user feedback while also facilitating a seamless comparison across multiple interview sessions. Our team systematically categorized and grouped similar ideas, observations, and pain points. This visual framework empowers us to discern patterns and overarching themes, providing a holistic view of user experiences.

Key Insights

  • A notable concern was that the unintuitive navigation requires excess scrolling in the current landing page layout. Feedback from our user interviews highlights a potential friction point that needs to be addressed to streamline user journeys and enhance overall usability
  • The user’s uncertainty about how they could interact with the organization through the site is also a concern. This points to a need for clearer communication and guidance, to ensure that users understand and can easily engage with the content.
  • We aim to keep the design clean and consistent as is represented in the original design. Leveraging this positive note while addressing navigation and engagement issues is the key in optimizing the overall user experience for the site.

Impactful Takeaways

  • The current site had an unintuitive navigation that required extra scrolling
  • Users were unsure how they could interact with the organization
  • User thought the design was clean and consistent

Test Analysis

Margins: Users expressed dissatisfaction with the spacing around elements on the interface. Insufficient margins made it difficult for them to navigate and interact comfortably with the content.

Card Size:Participants highlighted issues with the size of cards displaying information. Some found them too small, leading to difficulties in reading or interacting with the content effectively. Others suggested that larger cards would improve visibility and usability.

'Learn More' Links:Users pointed out that 'Learn More' links lacked specificity. They expressed a need for these links to direct them to a particular page or resource that provided relevant and detailed information about the topic they were interested in.

Forms Need Iterations:Feedback indicated that the forms used in the interface required refinement. Users encountered difficulties in completing the forms smoothly, suggesting a need for iteration to streamline the process and improve user experience.

Pain Points

  • margins
  • card size
  • 'learn more'
  • forms need iterations

Addressing these pain points identified through user interviews is crucial for enhancing the usability and effectiveness of the interface, ultimately leading to improved user satisfaction and engagement.

Reflections

Final Thoughts

The redesign journey for Visilant was more than just a visual transformation; it was a commitment to transforming lives. Shifting from a simplistic landing page to a comprehensive multi-page website was about creating an immersive experience for users passionate about accessible eye care and the mission to eliminate avoidable blindness.

We infused humanity into the organization by sharing compelling stories, humanizing the cause, and fostering a genuine emotional connection with our users. By turning the spotlight on the people behind the mission, we aimed to bridge the gap between the digital space and the real impact on lives.

The introduction of a donation feature serves as a vital bridge between empathy and action. Now, users can seamlessly contribute to the cause they believe in, directly supporting the noble efforts of Visilant.

Through thoughtful design choices, we can amplify the voice of those who need it most and drive impactful change.

Future Opportunities

Highlight Positive Outcomes and Patient Testimonials

  • Establish a dedicated section to showcase success stories and testimonials from patients who have experiences positive results from Visilant’s services.
  • Incorporate quotes, images, and videos from satisfied patients to convey authenticity and emotional resonance.
  • Ecourage patients to share their success stories on social media platforms and integrate social sharing buttons to maximize the reach of testimonials.

Launch a Blog Page

  • Develop a blog section for Visilant, where users can discover articles, updates, and insights pertaining to Visilant’s mission and services.
  • Provide options for users to subscribe to the blog.
  • Enable comments on blog entries to facilitate interactions among users.

Promote Donations

  • Extend the presence of the donation button across relevant pages like about, success stories, and blog pages.
  • Employ visuals and persuasive messaging to underscore the significance of donations.

With implementation of these approaches, Visilant can gain credibility, foster emotional connections with users, gain valuable information through content engagement, and stimulate support through donations.
